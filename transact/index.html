Step 1 : Buying a domain name

  1. Search for a domain and click Buy Now, a Paypal checkout window will open for you to complete the payment
  2. Our system will create a payment account using your email assigned to your paypal account, we will use this email to communicate with you
  3. after successful payment you will be redirected to an order confirmation page with steps to gain ownsership of the domain (step 2)

Step 2 : Gaining ownership of a purchased domain name (Important)

  1. We manage domains using Dynadot.com, to gain ownership of a purchased domain name you will have to request a domain pull from your Dynadot.com account, goto the following url : https://www.dynadot.com/domain/owner.html
  2. Enter the purchased domain in the section "Enter the domain name you want to become owner of".
  3. Click on the "Add to Order" button.
  4. Follow the "Checkout" process to submit your order.
    1. If you do not already have an existing Dynadot account, the system will prompt you to create a free account while your order is being checked out. Once you have submitted the domain pull order, an email will be sent to the owner of the domain asking them to authorize the move. Once they authorize the move, the domain will automatically be pulled from their account and placed in your account.
  5. We will confirm the transfer in 6 - 48 hours.
