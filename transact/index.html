Step 1 : Buying a domain name

  1. Search for a domain and click Buy Now, a checkout window will open to complete the payment using Paypal

  2. After successful payment completion, an email is sent to the email address associated with the Paypal account, this email communication is required to transfer ownership of the domain, this process is detailed below (Step 2)

Step 2 : Gaining ownership of a purchased domain name

  1. Domains are managed at Dynadot.com, to transfer ownership of a purchased domain name, a Dynadot.com account will be needed, if the buyer does not have one, a new free account can be created, to know more about account creation : How do I create a Dynadot.com account?

  2. As stated in Step 1, after successful payment completion an email is sent to the email address associated with the buyer's Paypal account requesting the buyer's Dynadot.com forum name to initiate the transfer

  3. A forum name is used to transfer a domain from one Dynadot.com account to another (Caution: do not confuse this with the Dynadot.com username), only the forum name is needed to transfer domain ownership, to know more about forum name : How do I find my Dynadot.com forum name?

  4. Buyer sends the Dynadot.com forum name to us by replying to the email, a domain transfer agent then initiate the ownership transfer within the next 6 - 48 hours

  5. Buyer will receive an email from Dynadot.com to accept the ownership transfer, on accepting, the buyer becomes the new owner of the domain, at this point step 2 is also complete and the transaction is marked as fulfilled
