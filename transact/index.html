Step 2 : Gaining ownership of a purchased domain name

  1. Domains are managed at Dynadot.com, to gain ownership of a purchased domain name, the buyer will have to create an account If they do not already have an existing Dynadot.com account
    1. How do I create a Dynadot.com account?
  2. As stated in Step 1, after successful payment completion an email is sent to the buyers email address associated with their Paypal account, this email requests the buyers Dynadot.com forum name
    1. Caution: A forum names is used to transfer a domain from one Dynadot.com account to another, do not confuse this with the Dynadot.com username, only the forum name is needed to transfer the domain ownership
    2. How do I find my Dynadot.com forum name?
  3. Buyer replies via email with the Dynadot.com forum name, a Tantra Media domain transfer agent will then initiate a domain ownership transfer within the next 6 - 48 hours
  4. Buyer will then receive an email from Dynadot.com to accept the domain ownership transfer, after accepting the buyer becomes the new owner of the domain and can start using the domain, at this point the entire transaction is marked as completed.
